Team History

Historic Moment

The Seattle Mariners joined the National Hockey League as an expansion team in 2021. Their second season was also the first time they ever made the posteason knocking off the defending champions the Avalanche in the first round.

The team hasn't been around long but players like Jordan Eberle, Philipp Grubauer, Yanni Gourde, Jared McCann, and Brandon 'Turbo' Tanev have all made a lasting impressions to Kraken fans.

Notable Achievements

  • Calder Memorial Trophy (2022-23 Matty Beniers)
  • Team Captain: Jordan Eberle (2024-Present)
  • 32 was retired on October 23, 2021, honoring the fans for 32,000 ticket deposits in one day and for being the NHL's newest (32nd) team

Getting There

Public transit is the fastest (and greenest) way to get to Climate Pledge Arena! All guests receive a free public transit pass alongside their ticket for every publicly ticketed event at the arena.

With expanded Light Rail service and easy parking at most stations, public transit is an easy, hassle-free way to get to your event.

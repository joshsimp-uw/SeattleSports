Team History

Historic Moments

Founded in 2007, Seattle Sounders FC has been a dominant force in MLS, winning multiple championships and consistently performing at the top level.

The club is known for its passionate fanbase, including the Emerald City Supporters, who create one of the best atmospheres in MLS.

Notable Achievements

  • MLS Cup Champions (2016, 2019)
  • U.S. Open Cup Champions (2009, 2010, 2011, 2014)
  • CONCACAF Champions League Winners (2022)
  • Western Conference Champions (2016, 2017, 2019, 2020, 2022)

Lumen Field

Stadium Information

  • Capacity: 37,722 (expandable to 69,000)
  • Opened: July 28, 2002
  • Address: 800 Occidental Ave S, Seattle, WA 98134
  • Features: Shared home with the Seattle Seahawks, known for its electric matchday atmosphere

Getting There

Lumen Field is easily accessible via Sound Transit Link Light Rail, Metro buses, and walking routes from downtown Seattle.

Parking is available at multiple nearby lots, but fans are encouraged to use public transit for ease of access.

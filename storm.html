Team History

Historic Moment

The Seattle Storm joined the WNBA as an expansion team in 2000. Since then they have won multiple championships and have had a very storied history in their short 25 year lifespan.

The team has had many promient players like Lauren Jackson and Breanna Stewart, but none come close to the legend of Sue Bird.

Notable Achievements

  • WNBA Championships (2004, 2010, 2018, 2020)
  • Commissioner's Cup Titles (2021)
  • Retired Numbers (10, 15)

Getting There

Public transit is the fastest (and greenest) way to get to Climate Pledge Arena! All guests receive a free public transit pass alongside their ticket for every publicly ticketed event at the arena.

With expanded Light Rail service and easy parking at most stations, public transit is an easy, hassle-free way to get to your event.

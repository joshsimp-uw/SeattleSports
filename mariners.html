Team History

Historic Moment

The Seattle Mariners joined Major League Baseball as an expansion team in 1977. Since then, they've created countless memorable moments, including their record-breaking 116-win season in 2001.

The team has featured legendary players like Ken Griffey Jr., Edgar Martinez, and Ichiro Suzuki.

Notable Achievements

  • AL West Division Champions (1995, 1997, 2001)
  • MLB Record: 116 wins in regular season (2001)
  • Seven MLB Hall of Fame inductees
  • Multiple All-Star Game representatives

T-Mobile Park

Stadium Information

  • Capacity: 47,929
  • Opened: July 15, 1999
  • Address: 1250 1st Ave S, Seattle, WA 98134
  • Features: Retractable roof for year-round baseball

Getting There

Located in Seattle's SoDo neighborhood, T-Mobile Park is easily accessible by public transportation, including Link Light Rail and Metro buses.

Parking is available in numerous lots surrounding the stadium.
